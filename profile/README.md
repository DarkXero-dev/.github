### About

I'm a Linux developer and open source maintainer based in the Arch ecosystem. I spend my time building tools, maintaining projects, and helping people get the most out of their Linux setups. My work centers around making things that are both functional and polished.

- Building and maintaining open source Linux projects
- Focused on KDE Plasma, system tooling, and package management
